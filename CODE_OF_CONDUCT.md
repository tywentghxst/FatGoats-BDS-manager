# Bedrock Server Manager Community Guidelines & Code of Conduct

Welcome to the Bedrock Server Manager community! Whether you are contributing on our GitHub repository or joining the discussion over on the r/BedrockServerManager subreddit, we want this to be a welcoming, helpful, and fun environment for everyone.

These guidelines apply equally to our Reddit community and all spaces managed within the Bedrock Server Manager GitHub project.

## Our Standards

Our goal is to foster an open and welcoming environment. We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

### 1. Be Respectful and Helpful
Treat others with respect. Disagreements happen, but we expect everyone to resolve them constructively. Remember that the community is here to help one another learn and improve their server setups.

### 2. Language and Tone
While we generally follow the spirit of official Minecraft community guidelines to ensure a safe environment, our approach is slightly more relaxed.
* **Profanity:** Occasional foul language is acceptable in casual conversation or when expressing frustration with a bug/system.
* **Zero Tolerance:** However, foul language **directed at another person**, insults, hate speech, or harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and will not be tolerated. Keep it cool and don't attack others.

### 3. Keep it Relevant
Please keep discussions focused on Bedrock Server Manager, Minecraft server administration, custom themes, plugins, and related technical topics. Avoid off-topic debates, spam, or blatant self-promotion unrelated to the project.

### 4. Search Before Asking
Before opening a new issue on GitHub or asking for help on Reddit, please take a moment to:
* Search the [Official Documentation](https://bedrock-server-manager.readthedocs.io/en/latest/).
* Search existing GitHub Issues.
* Use the search bar on the subreddit to see if your question has already been answered.

### 5. Provide Context When Asking for Help
When you need assistance, provide as much information as possible! Include error logs, your operating system, whether you use Docker, and steps to reproduce the issue. The more info you give, the faster someone can help you.

## Enforcement and Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders. You can report issues by:
* **GitHub:** Opening an issue (if public discussion is appropriate) or reaching out to the project maintainers directly.
* **Reddit:** Using the "Report" button on a post/comment or sending a Modmail to the r/BedrockServerManager moderators.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. Project maintainers and moderators have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for other behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
